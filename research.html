Research

Since I discovered the academic world, I've always wanted to pursue a career in research. I've worked as an intern in the laboratory LORIA in Nancy, France and made contributions to the research team I was involved in.

  • Neural Network Concept Interpreter

    This project is the result of my internship at LORIA. It is a software made in Python and tries to unveil whether the network has been able to use concepts to predict the output. My supervisor, Mathieu D'aquin, has carried forward the research, and the outcomes are documented in his paper published at the K-CAP '23 conference.

    Mathieu D’aquin. Finding Concept Representations in Neural Networks with Self-Organizing Maps. . K-CAP '23: Knowledge Capture Conference 2023, Dec 2023, Pensacola FL USA, France. pp.53-60, ⟨10.1145/3587259.3627551⟩. ⟨hal-04315074⟩
